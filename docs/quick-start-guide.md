## In 30 Seconds...

**What is this?**  
A application to record and track money spent on office/vehicle expenses.

**Who uses it?**  
Clerks enter expenses. Supervisors review monthly reports.

**How do I use it?**
1. Login with your username & password
2. Click "+ Add New Entry"
3. Enter bill details
4. Click "Save"
5. System validates the expense

**That's basically it!**

## Record an Expense (Step-by-Step)

### Step 1: Click "+ Add New Entry"

### Step 2: Fill Form
```
Date: [When you bought it]
Bill #: [Number from receipt] e.g., INV-001
Category: [Select one]
Description: [What you bought & why - minimum 10 words]
Amount: [Cost in LKR] e.g., 1500
```

### Step 3: Click "Save"

**Green ✓** = Saved successfully  
**Red ✗** = Error → Fix and retry  
**Yellow ⚠** = Warning → OK to proceed

---

## Important Rules

| Rule | Limit | What Happens |
|------|-------|--------------|
| **Monthly Budget** | LKR 25,000 | ❌ Cannot exceed |
| **Single Bill** | LKR 5,000 | ❌ Cannot exceed |
| **Amount** | Must be > 0 | ❌ Cannot be zero |
| **Bill #** | Unique per month | ❌ Cannot duplicate |
| **Date** | No future dates | ❌ Cannot be tomorrow |
| **Description** | Min 10 chars | ❌ Too short = error |
| **High-Value Bill** | > LKR 3,000 | ⚠️ Warning only |

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I enter a bill from yesterday?**  
A: Yes! You can enter any past date.

**Q: What if I made a mistake?**  
A: Click "View Entries" → Find it → Edit

**Q: Can I delete an entry?**  
A: Yes, but it stays in audit log.

**Q: What's the monthly limit?**  
A: LKR 25,000 per calendar month

**Q: Do I need internet?**  
A: No, works completely offline.
